What About Merbabu Mountain?

Mount Merbabu is a stratovolcano geographically located at 7.5° S and 110.4° E. Administratively, this mountain is situated within the Magelang Regency on the western slope, Boyolali Regency on the eastern and southern slopes, and Semarang Regency on the northern slope, all within Central Java Province.

What About Labuan Bajo?

Labuan Bajo is a hidden paradise located in the eastern part of Indonesia. This village is situated in the Komodo District, West Manggarai Regency, East Nusa Tenggara Province, which directly borders West Nusa Tenggara and is separated by the Sape Strait. Labuan Bajo is one of the five Super Priority Destinations currently being developed in Indonesia.
